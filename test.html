One AI brain, a whole digital hospital

Sentinel coordinates a network of specialist agents — mirroring global medical departments — to deliver structured, explainable guidance and triage while keeping clinicians in control at every step.[web:42][web:23]

Intelligent triage
Uses a 5‑level acuity framework inspired by modern emergency severity indexes to identify red flags, suggest urgency, and route patients to the right specialty in seconds.[web:60][web:63]
Specialist-grade coverage
Mirrors recognized medical specialties: Cardiology, Neurology, Oncology, Pediatrics, Psychiatry, Family Medicine, Emergency Medicine, Critical Care, Orthopedics, Dermatology, and more.[web:42][web:23]
Structured, explainable output
Every interaction yields a structured JSON care summary: symptom story, educational differentials, must-not-miss conditions, next steps, and red-flag education — ready for integration.[web:77]
Agentic orchestration
A coordinator agent works with department agents to handle multi-system problems, handing off cases across cardiology, nephrology, and critical care without losing context.
Safety-first behavior
Enforces strict AI disclaimers, avoids prescribing doses, surfaces uncertainty explicitly, and consistently advises in-person care and local emergency services when risk is high.[web:55][web:76]
Integration-ready
Exposes everything through an API for websites, apps, call centers, or EHR portals with auditability, role-based access control, and institution-specific guardrails.[web:77]
